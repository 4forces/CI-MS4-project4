About Us

At Upbeat Engineering, we love designing mechtronics products, teaching people about the joy of engineering and believe that being able to create hold your own creation is your hands is one of the greatest satisfaction in life.

Our main services include mechanical, electronics and vision systems product design and prototyping. And since we have so many spare parts and components lying around, we decided we should just sell our parts to help those in urgent need or parts.

To give back to society and encourage more people to love engineering, in our free time, we give lessons on engineering and design and design fun educational mechatronic products for kids.
